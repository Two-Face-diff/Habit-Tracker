Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start tracking a new habit?

To start tracking a new habit, go to the home page and click the "Add Habit" button. Fill in the habit details, set your frequency, and you're all set!

Can I track multiple habits at once?

Yes! You can track as many habits as you want. We recommend starting with 2-3 habits to build consistency before adding more.

How do streaks work?

Streaks count consecutive days you've completed your habit. Missing a day resets the streak, but don't worry - you can always start a new streak!

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information with third parties.
